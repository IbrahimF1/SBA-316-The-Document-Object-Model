How to Play

1) The Goal: Answer trivia questions correctly to build your High Score and Win Streak.

2) The Brave Path: Answer on your own! A correct answer awards 100 Points. A wrong answer costs nothing.

3) The Coward's Path: Stuck? Click "Leave it to fate" for a hint. However, your reward is cut to 50 Points.

4) The Penalty: Fate is high-stakes. If you leave it to fate and still answer incorrectly, you lose 100 Points.

5) The Fated Twist: As your Win Streak grows, fate becomes less reliable. Trust it at your own risk!
